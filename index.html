Learn about synesthesia

Synesthesia is a harmless neurological phenomenon (maybe linked to heredity), that results in senses merging with each other. For example, you can "see" soundes as different shapes or colors or you could associate a color with a specific letter.

Important disclaimer

This is an educational pre‑screen. It is not a medical test or diagnosis and is not a substitute for professional advice. Results are stored only in your browser unless you export them.
